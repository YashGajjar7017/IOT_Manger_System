Wireless OTA Guide

  1. 1
    Build firmware binary

    In Arduino IDE/PlatformIO, click Sketch > Export Compiled Binary to generate the .bin file.

  2. 2
    Connect WiFi AP

    Connect your computer's WiFi adapter to the gateway's broadcasted AP (SSID: ESP32_GATEWAY_XXXX).

  3. 3
    Upload and Flash

    Drag the compiled .bin file into the zone and trigger flashing. Progress is streamed live.

  4. 4
    Auto-Reboot

    Upon successful verification, the device will write to the active partition and automatically restart in less than 3 seconds.
